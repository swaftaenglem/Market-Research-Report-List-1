<p><p>The school building doors market primarily includes swing doors and tandem gates, each serving distinct purposes. Tandem gates, on the other hand, are multi-panel gates that slide open, suitable for larger openings or outdoor areas like sports fields. Both types prioritize safety, durability, and ease of use in educational environments, ensuring smooth movements within school premises.</p></p>
