<p><p>Online survey software and tools are categorized into two primary market types: on-premises and cloud-based. On-premises software is installed locally on a user's hardware, offering greater control over data security and customization but requiring significant IT resources for maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based solutions are hosted remotely, allowing users to access surveys from anywhere with an internet connection. These tools typically provide automatic updates, scalability, and lower upfront costs, making them ideal for businesses seeking flexibility and ease of use.</p></p>
