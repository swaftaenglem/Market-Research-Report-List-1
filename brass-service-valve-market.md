<p><ul><li>Balanced Service Valve</li><li>Unbalanced Service Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brass service valve market is categorized into two primary types: balanced and unbalanced service valves. Balanced service valves maintain equal pressure on both sides, ensuring stable flow conditions, making them suitable for high-demand systems. In contrast, unbalanced service valves have asymmetric pressure distribution, typically used in applications where flow direction and pressure vary significantly. Both types serve critical roles in fluid control, offering solutions that cater to diverse operational needs in industrial and residential settings.</p></p>
