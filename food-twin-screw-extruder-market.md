<p><strong>The Food Twin-screw Extruder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Parallel Co-rotating</li><li>Parallel Counter-rotating</li><li>Conical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The food twin-screw extruder market is segmented into three main types: parallel co-rotating, parallel counter-rotating, and conical extruders. Parallel co-rotating extruders feature two screws that rotate in the same direction, facilitating uniform mixing and high throughput. Parallel counter-rotating extruders have screws that rotate in opposite directions, enhancing material feeding and shear efficiency. Conical extruders feature a tapered design, promoting better control over the extrusion process and improving product quality. Each type caters to specific food processing requirements and applications.</p></p>
