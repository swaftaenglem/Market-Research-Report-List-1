<p><p>The Mechanical Splittable Microfiber market encompasses various types, primarily categorized as Nylon/Co-PET and PP/PET. Nylon/Co-PET fibers combine the durability and flexibility of nylon with poly(ethylene terephthalate) for enhanced performance in applications like cleaning products and textiles. Conversely, PP/PET fibers feature polypropylene’s lightweight and moisture-resistant properties alongside PET, making them suitable for diverse uses in filtration, automotive, and upholstery. These materials are valued for their low linting, superior water absorption, and effective cleaning capabilities.</p></p>
