<p><p>The Elevator Speed Limiters market comprises two primary types: One-Way Speed Limiters and Two-Way Speed Limiters. In contrast, Two-Way Speed Limiters monitor and control speed in both directions, providing comprehensive safety measures. These limiters are crucial for enhancing elevator safety by preventing overspeed situations, thus safeguarding passengers and equipment while ensuring regulatory compliance in various installations.</p></p>
