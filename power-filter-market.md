<p><p>The Power Filter Market is primarily divided into two types: Analog Filters and Digital Filters. Analog Filters use continuous signals to process and filter power quality issues in real-time, making them effective for specific applications with lower latency. In contrast, Digital Filters utilize discrete signals and advanced algorithms, allowing for enhanced flexibility, precision, and adaptability in various environments. Both types play crucial roles in improving electrical performance, reducing harmonics, and ensuring efficient power delivery across industries.</p></p>
