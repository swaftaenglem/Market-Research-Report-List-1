<p><p>Contact Management Systems (CMS) can be categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based CMS solutions are hosted on remote servers, offering easy accessibility, automatic updates, and scalability, making them ideal for businesses with remote teams. Conversely, on-premise CMS is installed locally on a company's servers, providing more control over data and customization but requiring more resources for maintenance and security. Each type caters to different organizational needs, budgets, and IT capabilities.</p></p>
