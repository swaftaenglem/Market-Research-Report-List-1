<p><p>The transdermal testosterone market primarily comprises two types: gels and patches. Testosterone gels are topical formulations that are absorbed through the skin, providing a convenient and adjustable dosage for patients. They typically offer rapid absorption but require careful application to prevent transfer to others. Testosterone patches, on the other hand, adhere to the skin and release a steady dose of the hormone over time. Both delivery methods aim to manage low testosterone levels effectively, catering to different patient preferences and needs.</p></p>
