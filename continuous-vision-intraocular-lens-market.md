<p><strong>The Continuous Vision Intraocular Lens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydrophobic Acrylic IOLs</li><li>Hydrophilic Acrylic IOLs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Continuous Vision Intraocular Lens (IOL) market includes primarily two types: hydrophobic acrylic IOLs and hydrophilic acrylic IOLs. Hydrophobic acrylic IOLs, made from water-repellent materials, offer excellent stability and reduced risk of posterior capsule opacification, making them popular for premium cataract surgeries. In contrast, hydrophilic acrylic IOLs, which are water-absorbing, provide enhanced biocompatibility and are generally easier to handle during implantation. Both types aim to improve visual outcomes and patient satisfaction following cataract procedures.</p></p>
