<p><strong>The Airflow Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vane Airflow Sensor</li><li>Karman Vortex Airflow Sensor</li><li>Hot Wire Airflow Sensor</li><li>Hot Film Airflow Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Airflow Sensor market comprises various sensor types, each suited for specific applications. Vane airflow sensors use rotating blades to measure air velocity, ideal for high-flow environments. Karman vortex sensors detect flow patterns by capturing vortices, providing high accuracy in various conditions. Hot wire sensors measure changes in electrical resistance due to airflow, commonly used in low-velocity applications. Hot film sensors operate similarly but use a thin film to enhance sensitivity, suitable for delicate measurements in diverse environments.</p></p>
