<p><ul><li>Resistive Touch Screen</li><li>Capacitive Touch Screen</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Touch Controller IC market is primarily divided into two types: resistive and capacitive touch screens. Resistive touch screens detect touch through pressure applied to the surface, making them suitable for environments requiring durability and precision. They are often used in industrial and medical applications. Capacitive touch screens, on the other hand, sense touch through the electrical properties of the human body, offering higher sensitivity and multi-touch capabilities. They are commonly found in smartphones, tablets, and modern consumer electronics.</p></p>
