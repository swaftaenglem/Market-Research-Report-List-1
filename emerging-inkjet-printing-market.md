<p><p>The emerging inkjet printing market is primarily divided into two main types: demand inkjet printing technology and continuous inkjet printing technology. Demand inkjet printing, also known as drop-on-demand, releases ink only when needed, offering precision and versatility for various applications like textiles and packaging. In contrast, continuous inkjet printing maintains a constant flow of ink, making it ideal for high-speed industrial applications such as coding and marking. Both technologies are advancing to meet increasing demands for customization and efficiency across industries.</p></p>
