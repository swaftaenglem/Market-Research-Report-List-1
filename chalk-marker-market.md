<p><p>The chalk marker market is segmented into two main types based on nib design: bullet nib and chisel nib. Bullet nib markers offer a rounded tip that provides consistent, bold lines, ideal for detailed writing or artwork. In contrast, chisel nib markers feature a flat edge that allows for varied line thickness, suitable for both fine details and broad strokes. These markers are popular in schools, businesses, and arts and crafts due to their versatility, ease of use, and vibrant colors.</p></p>
