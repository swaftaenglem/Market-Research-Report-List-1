<p><p>String solar PV inverters are categorized into low-frequency and high-frequency inverters based on their operational characteristics. Low-frequency inverters typically utilize transformers, offering robust performance and reliability but may be bulkier and less efficient. In contrast, high-frequency inverters employ transformerless designs, resulting in lighter, more compact units with higher efficiency and faster response times. Both types serve to convert solar energy into usable electricity, catering to different installation environments and energy requirements in the growing solar market.</p></p>
