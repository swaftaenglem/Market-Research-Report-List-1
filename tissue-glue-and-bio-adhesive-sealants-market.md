<p><p>The tissue glue and bio-adhesive sealants market encompasses various types of sealants used for wound closure and tissue adhesion. Thrombin-based sealants activate coagulation to rapidly stop bleeding, and fibrin sealants mimic the final stages of clotting. Gelatin-based sealants provide a biodegradable option, while other proprietary formulations cater to specific application needs. Each type offers unique benefits tailored to surgical requirements.</p></p>
