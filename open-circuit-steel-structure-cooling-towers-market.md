Free Ventilation cooling towers rely on natural airflow to dissipate heat, utilizing ambient wind conditions for efficient cooling. In contrast, Mechanical Ventilation cooling towers use fans or blowers to enhance airflow and improve heat exchange, ensuring consistent performance regardless of external conditions. Both types serve distinct applications in industrial and commercial settings, providing effective thermal management solutions.</p></p>
