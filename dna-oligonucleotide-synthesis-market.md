<p><p>The DNA oligonucleotide synthesis market encompasses various product types, each serving distinct purposes. Primers are short sequences used in PCR and sequencing. Probes are labeled oligonucleotides essential for detection and quantification of specific DNA or RNA sequences. Intermediate-scale synthesis oligos cater to medium-scale research and development, while large-scale synthesis oligos are produced in bulk for commercial applications. Additionally, linkers and adaptors facilitate the connection between different molecular fragments, playing a crucial role in genetic engineering and molecular biology.</p></p>
