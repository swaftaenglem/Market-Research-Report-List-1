Silver bromide is critical for traditional film photography, as it is a light-sensitive compound that captures images. Sodium sulfide is used in developing processes, while selenium dioxide is used for toning prints and enhancing image stability. Together, these chemicals facilitate high-quality imaging.</p></p>
