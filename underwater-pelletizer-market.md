<p><strong>The Underwater Pelletizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Strand Palletizing System</li><li>Underwater Palletizing System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The underwater pelletizer market comprises two main types: the strand pelletizing system and the underwater pelletizing system. </p><p>The strand pelletizing system involves extruding polymer strands that are cooled and cut into pellets, mainly used for thermoplastics. </p><p>In contrast, the underwater pelletizing system entails extruding material into water, where it is cooled and cut into pellets directly in a submerged environment, resulting in more uniform pellets. Both systems cater to varying production needs and specific applications in the plastics industry.</p></p>
