<p><p>The neonatal jaundice phototherapy machine market comprises two main types: single surface and double surface phototherapy. Single surface phototherapy involves placing the infant under a light source on one side, primarily targeting the skin exposed to the light. In contrast, double surface phototherapy utilizes light sources on both the top and bottom, maximizing light exposure and enhancing the treatment's effectiveness. Both types aim to reduce bilirubin levels in newborns, ensuring faster recovery and improved health outcomes.</p></p>
