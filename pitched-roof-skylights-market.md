<p><p>The pitched roof skylights market is categorized into two main types: fixed type and activity type. Fixed type skylights are designed for passive light transmission, offering a stable and unmovable structure that enhances natural lighting without ventilation. Activity type skylights, on the other hand, provide functionality beyond light, allowing for operable features that promote airflow and regulate indoor temperatures. Both types serve to improve aesthetic appeal and energy efficiency in various architectural designs, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
