<p><ul><li>Direct Fired Ceramics</li><li>Electric Stewed Ceramics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ceramic cookware sets market comprises two primary types: direct fired ceramics and electric stewed ceramics. Direct fired ceramics are designed for use on open flames or gas stoves, offering high durability and excellent heat retention. These pieces are ideal for traditional cooking methods. In contrast, electric stewed ceramics are tailored for electric cooking appliances, providing convenience and versatility for slow cooking and simmering. Both types are valued for their non-toxic properties, even heat distribution, and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
