<p><p>Point of Sale (POS) software can be categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based POS systems operate online, allowing users to access their data from any device with an internet connection, offering scalability and real-time updates. In contrast, on-premises POS solutions are installed locally on hardware within a business, providing greater control and security but often requiring higher upfront costs and maintenance. Each type caters to different business needs, budgets, and operational preferences.</p></p>
