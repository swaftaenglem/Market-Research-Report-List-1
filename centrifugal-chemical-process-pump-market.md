<p><strong>The Centrifugal Chemical Process Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>End Suction</li><li>Back Pull</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The centrifugal chemical process pump market is categorized primarily into two types: end suction and back pull-out pumps. End suction pumps feature a horizontal design where fluid enters axially, making them ideal for lower-viscosity fluids and straightforward installations. Back pull-out pumps allow easy access to internal components without disturbing the piping, enhancing maintenance efficiency. Both types serve critical roles in transferring chemicals across various industries, with specific advantages tailored to different operational needs and fluid characteristics.</p></p>
