<p><p>The flat slings market comprises primarily two types: metal slings and high-strength fiber slings. In contrast, high-strength fiber slings, typically made from synthetic materials like nylon or polyester, provide lightweight flexibility and excellent load distribution, making them suitable for environments sensitive to surface damage.
